APERTURE CLOUD SECURITY OVERVIEW
Prepared for Northstar Bank vendor diligence

Summary
Aperture Cloud provides a multi-tenant SaaS platform with regional deployment controls, SSO, SCIM, and audit logging. The Northstar Bank pilot is proposed in the EU region.

Identity and access
- SAML 2.0 single sign-on is supported for enterprise workspaces.
- SCIM provisioning is available on the Enterprise plan.
- Administrative users must enroll in MFA.
- Break-glass accounts are restricted, monitored, and reviewed quarterly.

Logging and monitoring
- Administrative actions are logged.
- Authentication events are logged.
- Audit logs are retained for thirteen months in the standard enterprise configuration.
- High-risk events trigger pager notifications to the security on-call rotation.

Encryption
- TLS 1.2 or higher is required in transit.
- Customer content is encrypted at rest using AES-256.

Application security
- External penetration testing is performed annually.
- Critical vulnerabilities are targeted for remediation within seven calendar days.
- High severity vulnerabilities are targeted for remediation within fourteen calendar days.

Business continuity
- Service backups are encrypted.
- Production databases are replicated across availability zones.
- The target recovery time objective is four hours.
- The target recovery point objective is fifteen minutes.

Data location
For Northstar Bank, the recommended production deployment is the EU region. Support access from outside the EU requires ticketed approval and is limited to exceptional troubleshooting cases.

Third-party services
Key subprocessors include cloud infrastructure, email delivery, and monitoring vendors. Changes to subprocessors are announced in advance through the customer trust portal.
