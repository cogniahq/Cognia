SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT
Aperture Cloud Enterprise

1. Availability commitment
Provider will make the production service available 99.95 percent of the time in each calendar month, excluding scheduled maintenance, emergency maintenance, and customer-caused outages.

2. Scheduled maintenance
Scheduled maintenance windows will generally occur on Sundays between 01:00 and 03:00 UTC. Provider will attempt to give at least five days' notice for scheduled maintenance expected to affect customer access.

3. Support severity definitions
Severity 1 means a production outage or material loss of core functionality with no workaround.
Severity 2 means severe degradation of service with a viable workaround.
Severity 3 means a non-critical defect or general support request.

4. Response targets
- Severity 1: fifteen-minute acknowledgement, hourly updates
- Severity 2: one-hour acknowledgement
- Severity 3: one business day acknowledgement

5. Service credits
If monthly uptime falls below 99.95 percent but remains at or above 99.0 percent, Customer receives a 5 percent service credit.
If monthly uptime falls below 99.0 percent but remains at or above 98.0 percent, Customer receives a 10 percent service credit.
If monthly uptime falls below 98.0 percent, Customer receives a 25 percent service credit.

6. Disaster recovery commitments
Provider targets an RTO of four hours and an RPO of fifteen minutes for the primary production environment.

7. Exclusions
This SLA does not apply to beta features, unsupported configurations, third-party network outages beyond Provider control, or issues caused by Customer systems or identity provider misconfiguration.
