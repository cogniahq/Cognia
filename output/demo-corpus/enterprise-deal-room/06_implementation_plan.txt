IMPLEMENTATION PLAN
Northstar Bank x Aperture Cloud
Prepared by Solution Delivery

Project objective
Launch the Northstar Bank production workspace for procurement and legal operations with SSO, SCIM, document migration for the pilot corpus, and baseline governance controls.

Key dates
- Project kickoff: June 10, 2026
- Identity workshop: June 12, 2026
- SSO and SCIM configuration complete: June 19, 2026
- Pilot document migration complete: June 24, 2026
- Security sign-off meeting: June 26, 2026
- UAT start: June 29, 2026
- Go-live readiness review: July 8, 2026
- Production go-live target: July 15, 2026

Milestones
Milestone 1: Kickoff and stakeholder alignment
Milestone 2: SAML SSO configured in test and production tenants
Milestone 3: SCIM provisioning validated with the bank identity team
Milestone 4: Pilot corpus uploaded and access model confirmed
Milestone 5: Security and legal approvals complete
Milestone 6: End-user enablement and go-live

Dependencies
- Northstar Bank must provide IdP metadata by June 13, 2026.
- Northstar Bank must approve the subprocessor list before production cutover.
- Provider must share SOC 2 Type II report and penetration testing executive summary before the security sign-off meeting.

Go-live gate
Production go-live requires written approval from the bank's information security lead, the procurement workstream lead, and the executive sponsor. No go-live will occur before SSO is validated and the security sign-off meeting is complete.
